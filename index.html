Biography

Dr. Lily Truong is running for the Texas State Representative District 149 position in 2020. She currently serves the community as a Trustee for the Alief Independent School District for the second term in a row. She has lived in Alief ISD area since 1977 and at her current home address in the Texas House District 149 for 21 years.

Dr. Truong graduated from Alief Hastings High School. She studied Biology at University of Houston. She went on to earn a Doctor of Philosophy in Natural Medicine degree from Texas Chiropractic College through Kingdom College of Natural Health.

Dr. Truong's primary profession revolves around providing assistance to families with children diagnosed with Autism. Her passion for social service is reflected in the over 21 years she has devoted to working with families and students. Additionally, she provides administrative support to the Harris County Department of Adult Education 3 nights a week in the ESL and GED evening classes program. In her free time, she enjoys doing volunteer work throughout the community. Dr. Truong is a founder and past president of the University of Houston Asian Alumni Association. She believes that a key to unlocking a better community life is through Education. She advocates for improving English literacy and proficiency in the community. She is strong on Education.

Dr. Truong is a member of the Ascension Chinese Mission, a Catholic church, in Alief. She previously served as a Special Needs Pastoral Council Member for 17 years. Currently she is a member of the Finance Committee at the church.

Dr. Truong is married to her husband of over 37 years, John Truong. Together, they have three children, Michael, Anthony and Angela. She was recently promoted to grandmother with the birth of her first grandson, Carter, and first granddaughter, Mia, whom she adores and loves very much.
